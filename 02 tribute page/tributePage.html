Osagyefo Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

The Man Who Led Gold Coast To Independence

A timeline of Dr. Kwame Nkrumah:

  • April 7, 1909: Born in Nkroful, Gold Coast (now Ghana).
  • 1935: Graduated from Lincoln University in Pennsylvania with a degree in economics and sociology .
  • 1945: Earned a Master of Science degree in education from the University of Pennsylvania .
  • 1947: Became the general secretary of the United Gold Coast Convention (UGCC) .
  • 1949: Founded the Convention People's Party (CPP).
  • 1951: Elected to the Legislative Assembly and appointed leader of government business .
  • 1952: Arrested and imprisoned for sedition .
  • 1954: Released from prison and elected to the Legislative Assembly .
  • 1957: Became the first prime minister of Ghana after the country gained independence from Britain on March 6th .
  • 1960: Became the first president of Ghana after the country became a republic on July 1st .
  • 1964: A constitutional amendment made Ghana a one-party state with Nkrumah as president for life of both the nation and its party .
  • 1972: Died on April 27th in Bucharest, Romania while receiving medical treatment for cancer .
